Entertainment

Entertainment down into these sub-categories:

  • Restaurant
  • Gym membership
  • Concerts/Festivals
  • Sports games
  • Movies
  • Books

Most of these expenses are not needs, but wants. It is a good idea to leave this category for last when figuring out how much money you can allocate to it in your budget. Remember, it is okay to treat yourself once in a while!
