Scholarships

Scholarships provide an opportunity for many people to earn an education. Without assistance from an outside source, students may have trouble paying for the degree they need in order to enter the workforce and become a contributing member of society.

Reasons to Apply for Scholarships:

1. The cost of living while in college is increasing.
2. State support for students has decreased and will continue to.
3. Loans can lead to debt. Click here to learn how to manage your debt as a post-graduate.
4. Many of us are already in debt, and owe money for things such as homes, cars, family expenses, etc.

Knowledge is powerful. Aspire and achieve. As a part of financial literacy, learn about scholarships which can help you manage your money and pay for your tuition in college.
