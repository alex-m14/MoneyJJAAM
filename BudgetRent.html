Rent and Utilities

Rent is one of the biggest expenses to pay after college. To save the most money, staying with your parents is the most ideal until you save enough to move out. However, this is not possible for many post graduates.

Another option is moving in with roommates. Dividing the rent evenly makes it more affordable.

Try and find an apartment or house where some or all utilities are included.

Services like Tiller can help you track your spending and puts all of your expenses in one place.
