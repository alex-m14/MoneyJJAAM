What is the AC^2 Program?

AC^2 stands for the AMP and CSTEP Community at SUNY New Paltz. The program is dedicated to assisting student of Latinx or Black backgrounds who also exhibit significant financial need. One of their resources includes the "Lending Library", which guarentees each student at least one free STEM textbook for the semester. They also offer scholarships and awards for those that are active in the program.

Stop by Science Hall 214 with any questions!
What is the Educational Opportunity Program?

EOP provides academic and financial support for those who do not meet the general admission criteria , but have the potential to earn a college degree. Most students admitted to the program are first generation college students.

Common Misconception:

This program is not designated for Black/Latinx students. It is for all students who meet the academic and financial requirements.

What is the Scholar's Mentorship Program?

SMP is a multicultural program, funded by the Black Studies Department, that addresses the educational disparities of historically underrepresented students of SUNY New Paltz. First-Year students are assigned a both a peer and faculty mentor to help ease their transition to New Paltz. They also provide scholarship awards and academic recognition awards throughout the year.
