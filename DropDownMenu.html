Tips for Saving Money After College

Budgeting is a useful concept. To start, add up the essentials, like your rent, utilities, groceries, transportation, student loans and a car loan. This will let show you the amount of money you have left to spend each month and what is the most expensive.

The Budgeting Help drop down bar provides more information on how to save money by splitting it up into different categories.
